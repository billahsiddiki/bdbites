Stress Test Results — 7 Critical Fixes Read First

Fix #1: WhatsApp is BLOCKED in Bangladesh The original blueprint recommended WhatsApp Channels. As of March 2026, WhatsApp remains blocked in Bangladesh. Multiple sources confirm BD is among 12 countries blocking WhatsApp access. Replaced with: Telegram channels (available in BD, growing rapidly) + Facebook Messenger broadcast via ManyChat.
Fix #2: NapoleonCat is $79/mo, NOT $32/mo The original blueprint quoted $32/mo. Verified pricing: Standard plan starts at $79/mo for 5 profiles. For 20 accounts: significantly more expensive. Replaced with: Publer's built-in comment management (included in Business plan) + Meta Graph API direct comment monitoring via n8n (free). Saves ~$79/mo.
Fix #3: Publer Has DAILY POST LIMITS Professional plan caps at 12 FB posts/day and 15 IG posts/day per profile. At your volume targets, you WILL hit these. Solution: Publer Business plan ($21/mo base + per-profile) removes some limits. For Facebook/Instagram, supplement with direct Meta Business Suite API posting via n8n (free, no limits on Pages you own). For media companies, Publer can waive limits — contact them.
Fix #4: Hetzner Price Increase April 1, 2026 Hetzner announced 20-30% price increases effective April 1, 2026. CX32 (4 vCPU, 8GB RAM) goes from €6.30 to approximately €8.19/mo (~$9). Updated all infrastructure costs accordingly.
Fix #5: Bannerbear 1,000 Credits/mo Won't Cover Volume At 100+ images/day, you'll exhaust the $49/mo Automate plan (1,000 credits) in 10 days. Options: Scale plan at $149/mo (10,000 credits) or switch to Orshot at $30/mo for 3,000 renders. Recommended: Start with Orshot, upgrade to Bannerbear Scale when revenue covers it.
Fix #6: Apify Starter Plan is $29/mo, NOT $49/mo The original quoted $49/mo. Current verified price: $29/mo Starter with pay-as-you-go compute. Updated cost calculations. However, the $29 includes only $29 of platform credits — high-volume scraping may exceed this. Budget $40-60/mo for Apify realistically.
Fix #7: Claude Sonnet 4.6 Pricing Confirmed Claude Sonnet 4.6: $3/MTok input, $15/MTok output. For scoring + writing ~3,000 items/day with average 500 input + 300 output tokens per call: estimated $65-100/mo. This is accurate and cost-effective. DeepSeek V3 overflow at $0.14/MTok for bulk volume remains viable.

Platform Algorithms (2026)

72.6M BD users — LARGEST AUDIENCE. 2026 priority: Meaningful interactions > comments > shares > reactions. NEVER use link posts — post image, link in comments. Respond to EVERY comment in first hour. Go Live during breaking news for massive priority boost. Optimal caption: 100–250 words.

9.9M BD users — GROWTH PLATFORM (+46% YoY). 2026: Reels > Carousels > Single images. Carousels: 1.4× more reach than single images. Max 3–5 hashtags (reduced hashtag weight, increased keyword weight). Stories 8–10× per day with polls. Caption keywords are now searchable (Instagram SEO).

46.5M BD users — VIRAL ENGINE. 2026: Completion rate > shares > comments > likes. Sweet spot: 15–45 sec news, 30–90 entertainment. First 1 second MUST have visual movement. Text-on-screen critical — most BD users watch without sound. Never post identical content to TikTok and Instagram.

44.6M BD users — MONETIZATION PLATFORM. Shorts threshold: 1K subs + 10M Shorts views in 90 days. Remove ALL watermarks from cross-posted content. Shorts algorithm favors new channels. Add end screen: "Subscribe for daily Bangladesh news in 30 seconds."

The 7 Kill Risks

#1: Bad AI Comment Goes Viral — One tone-deaf reply screenshotted 50K times = brand finished. Phase 1: NO auto-replies. AI drafts, you review top 20 daily. Never auto-reply to political/religious topics. Ever.
#2: Publishing False Information — One wrong fact on Day 5 = nobody trusts you on Day 50. Tier 1 sources (Reuters, AP, Prothom Alo): 5 min. Social media claims: NEVER publish as fact. Reverse image search EVERY news image.
#3: Platform Account Suspension — Use only official APIs. Never use unofficial automation (Jarvee etc). Remove ALL watermarks from cross-posted YouTube content. Stagger posting times across channels.
#4: CIB Detection — All Channels Banned Together — Stagger posting times (Prohor at :00/:30, Dhaka Brief at :10/:40, etc). Different ad accounts per brand. Different visual templates. Never share payment methods across Meta Business accounts.
#5: Bangla Writing Sounds Like AI — Generate 50 samples. Show to 5 BD friends WITHOUT telling them it's AI. If more than 1 in 5 spots it, rewrite prompts. $0 test prevents $50K mistake.
#6: You Burn Out in Month 2 — Minimum: ~45 min/day Months 1–3, dropping to ~20 min/day by Month 6. Spot-check posts, review flagged comments, check alerts. Not optional.
#7: Publer Daily Limits Silently Cap Your Output — Professional plan: 12 FB/15 IG posts per day per profile. At 20 accounts × 5-10 posts each = you'll hit limits fast. Use Publer for TikTok/YouTube/X scheduling, and Meta Business Suite API (free, no limits) for Facebook/Instagram via n8n.
