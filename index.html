Platform Algorithms (2026)

LARGEST BD AUDIENCE. Priority: Meaningful interactions > comments > shares. NEVER use link posts — image + link in comments. Go Live for breaking news (massive boost). Respond to EVERY comment in first hour. Optimal caption: 100–250 words. Post images with long captions, not links.

GROWTH PLATFORM (+20% YoY). Reels > Carousels > Single images. Carousels: 1.4× more reach. Max 3–5 hashtags (keyword weight increased). Stories 8–10× per day with polls. Caption keywords are searchable (Instagram SEO). Post 3–5 feed posts per day.

VIRAL ENGINE. Completion rate > shares > comments > likes. Sweet spot: 15–45 sec news, 30–90 entertainment. First 1 second MUST have movement. Text-on-screen critical — most BD users watch muted. Never identical content to IG. Post 3–8 times/day.

MONETIZATION PLATFORM. Shorts: 1K subs + 10M views in 90 days. Remove ALL watermarks. Shorts algo favors new channels. Cross-post TikTok content (clean, no watermark). End screen: "Subscribe for daily BD news in 30 seconds."

ELITE INFLUENCE. Journalists, politicians, diplomats. Thread format for explainers. 10–15 tweets/day. Quote-tweet viral BD tweets with analysis. "Pressure group" influence among policy circles.

#2: Publishing False Information One wrong fact Day 5 = nobody trusts Day 50. Tier 1 sources: 5 min. Social claims: NEVER as fact. Reverse image search EVERY news image.
#3: Platform Suspension Official APIs only. Never Jarvee or unofficial tools. Remove ALL watermarks from cross-posted YT content. Stagger posting times across channels.

#5: Bangla Sounds Like AI Generate 50 samples, show 5 BD friends WITHOUT saying it's AI. If 1+ spots it, rewrite prompts. $0 test prevents $50K mistake.
#6: Burnout Month 2 Minimum: ~45 min/day Months 1–3, dropping to ~20 min/day Month 6+. Not optional.
